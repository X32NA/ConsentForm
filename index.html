Introduction

You have been chosen to take part in a survey as part of a research project aimed at gathering insights to understand the viability of innovative interventions for enhancing mental well-being in young adults. This research is conducted by Dr. Saira Hanif Soroya, an Associate Professor at the Southern Connecticut State University, New Haven, CT.

Key Elements

The objectives of this study are to:

·  examine the feasibility (Resources, Readiness, Skills, & Risks) of using digital intervention in the identification and reduction of mental health challenges among the young generation.

·  propose contextual requirements for digital intervention development to help young patients with mental health challenges.

Purpose

The main thesis of this study revolves around the idea that digital interventions can be effective in addressing mental health challenges and social stigma in low-income countries. The plan aims to collect basic data to examine the feasibility and challenges of developing mental health-supporting interventions.
